Speed Gearing Guide

note:

Start with 3 and 4 liner base with speed on them. Dont level 3 liners to 4 liners in an attempt to get speed as the 4th. Not really worth your time.

3-Liner Tree

Roll 3-liner pieces to level 6

By +6

Hit speed twice

You are already at 12+ speed. Follow the green lane.

By +6

Hit speed once

Needs a confirmation roll at +9 before you sink more gold.

By +6

Missed speed twice

Scrap unless the off-stats match a hyper niche build.

4-Liner Tree

Roll 4-liner pieces to level 6

By +6

Hit speed twice

You are already at 12+ speed. Follow the green lane.

By +6

Hit speed once

Needs a confirmation roll at +9 before you sink more gold.

By +6

Missed speed twice

Scrap unless the off-stats match a hyper niche build.

Green = keep rolling.

Amber = check after every roll.

Red = recycle.
